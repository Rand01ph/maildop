Our Company

Built with love by passionate people!

How we are?

Maildop is an easy-to-use email marketing tool that simplifies how you create, send, and track your email newsletters. Our team has drawn from many years of industry experience to create an email marketing tool that provides the highest returns for small businesses. We built Maildop to make email marketing accessible for everyone, on both a price and expertise level. It's the easy-going email marketing tool with the best customer support. Simple and clear.

What we focus on?

Using our experience in the local business environment, we explicitly developed Maildop to match the unique needs of businesses operating all over the world. Furthermore, our servers are deployed in American to ensure unmatched deliverability and speed.

What will the future bring?

We understand that sometimes email marketing might seem to be a bit scary and complex, but it's not. Maildop eliminates the complexity and makes email marketing simple and understandable. Email marketing is a very easy yet powerful tool to connect with your customers and we see that many small businesses in world start to understand this marketing channel. We want to digitally empower those businesses so they can succeed.

I'm grateful we made it that far and continue strong! Thank you all for supporting us!

Qinhui Zeng, CEO
